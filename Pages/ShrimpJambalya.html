Shrimp Jambalaya

A delicious creole dish that will make you wanna slap yo mamma!

This recipe features all the savory ingredients you'll need to make your mouth water. The recipe substitutes white rice for brown in order to keep things light and fresh. Also, who doesn't love a good shrimp recipe?? Don't worry. If you are allergic you can always substitute the shrimp for chicken.
