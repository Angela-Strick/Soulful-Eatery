Grilled Sweet Potato Wedges

Pair these bad boys with a lean turkey burger and your next cookout will be a hit!

This recipe is exactly what you need this summer. These wedges serve as a fantastic side dish for any summer get together.

Ingredients for Wedges

  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • ground pepper
  • kosher salt
  • 1 lime
  • 1 teaspoon of lime zest
  • 1 teaspoon of harlssa mix
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425ยบ F. Cut the sweet potatoes in half and then cut each half into four equal sized wedges. Arrange the sweet potato wedges on a rimmed baking sheet and drizzle with the olive oil. Season the sweet potato wedges with salt and pepper.
  2. Place sweet potatoes into the preheated oven and roast until the potatoes are fork tender, about 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and set aside
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium or place a cast-iron stovetop grill pan over medium heat.
  4. Grill until golden brown on all sides about 2 minutes per side. Transfer to a platter
  5. Mix together salt, pepper, lime juice, zest, maple syrup, and chili powder; add oil to the mixture slowly and mix well. Brush mixture on sweet potatoes, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve immediately.

Enjoy!
